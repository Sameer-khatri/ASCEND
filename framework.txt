Final Daily Training Protocol
Pillar 0 — Self Awareness (5 minutes, every night)
Write three things:

One pattern you noticed in yourself today
One moment you reacted badly
One moment you performed well

Then one line — what did you learn across all pillars today?

Pillar 1 — Mind / Knowledge
One thing learned today — not just consumed, actually applied.
One sentence only: how did you or will you use it in real life?

Pillar 2 — Body / Physical
Keep your running.
After every session — 5 minutes. No phone.
Breathing, posture, recovery. Connect your mind to your body consciously.

Pillar 3 — Emotional Intelligence
Two logs:

One moment you were triggered negatively — what happened, why it hit you, what you did, what you should have done
One moment you handled something well — what was different about that moment


Pillar 4 — Social
One genuine conversation — not small talk, full attention.
Afterwards write:

One thing you noticed about them you would've missed otherwise
One insecurity that surfaced during the interaction and why it arose


Pillar 5 — Strategic Thinking
One decision reviewed honestly:

What did you want
What did you decide and why
What actually happened
Was it correct or not — call yourself out if wrong

Now Let's Name These Features Properly
So when you build — you know exactly what each piece does:
1. Memory Confrontation
System references your past entries and asks follow up questions. Never lets you start fresh like nothing happened.
2. Challenge Mode
Random days — no logging. Instead a real scenario thrown at you. You respond. System judges your reasoning across whichever pillar the scenario tests.
3. Pillar Health Dashboard
Six pillars. Each with a real score based on your entries. Visual. Updated daily. No hiding from which pillar you're neglecting.
4. Brutal Feedback Engine
No sugarcoating in responses. If your strategic thinking entry shows you made the same mistake three times — it tells you directly. No encouragement without evidence.
